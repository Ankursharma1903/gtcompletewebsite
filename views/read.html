About Us

Gaming Titans

The Gaming Titans is the first gaming society of GTBIT , Founded on 9TH MARCH 2022

INCHARGE : MS. JASLEEN KAUR
STUDENTS LEAD : HARSIMRAN SINGH , MANMEET SINGH

GAMING TITANS : THE GAMING SOCIETY OF GTBIT is dedicated in providing a safe evnvironment for members to relax and enjoy multiplayer games with like-minded people.The society welcomes gamers of all levels to join for a stress-free, fun experience - playing cooperative and competitive games. Apart from this we aim at providing platform to members where they can express their gaming skills efficiently and to bring those with common interests in gaming together. We offer the chance to meet gaming needs where you can play games and socialize along the way, be it through mobiles, consoles or PCs. Our society not only supports gaming but also helps develop other skills like event management , video editing , designing and other technical stuff required in a professional life. In today’s time gaming is not just a “waste of time” as people think it is, When done in a creative and structured manner its a lot more than people can think. E-sports is a large upcoming field which can be taken up as a career and that’s also a part of what we do here. Playing competitions at a professional level not only encourages the members to work harder but also teach them about the future of E-sports in India. Taking the moto, “ WE WIN , WE SURVIVE!” forward and close to our heart we continue to work for the upliftment of the members of this society and help them achieve their goals
